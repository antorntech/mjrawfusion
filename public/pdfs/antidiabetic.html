Anti-Diabetic APIS are tailored to address diabetes mellitus by regulating blood sugar levels. These active pharmaceutical ingredients function through diverse mechanisms, including enhancing insulin sensitivity, promoting insulin release, suppressing glucose production in the liver, or delaying carbohydrate absorption in the intestines.
